# Nido — Personas

> **How to fill this out:** Specific archetypes, not generic roles. Each persona should be someone you could imagine in real detail — name them, give them a context, make them feel like a person. Aim for 3-5 personas. More than that and they blur. Fewer than 3 and you're missing edge cases.
>
> Duplicate the persona block below for each persona. Delete the prompt text once you've answered.

---

## Persona 1: {Name}, {one-line description}

> **Prompt — name and one-line:** Give them a name and a single sentence. "Marisol, founder of a 6-year-old ecovillage in Costa Rica with 18 families, running it as a side job while her real job pays the bills." If you can't name them, they're not specific enough.

### Their day

> **Prompt:** What does their Tuesday morning look like? Where are they when they open Nido? On their phone walking to the garden? On their laptop after the kids are asleep? Describe the context, not just the actions.

### What they're afraid of

> **Prompt:** Not technical concerns — emotional ones. "She's afraid of being the person who introduced another tool that didn't stick and burned the community's patience." That fear shapes every adoption decision.

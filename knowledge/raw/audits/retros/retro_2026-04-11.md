## Challenges

- **Volume of findings** — 18 audits produced hundreds of individual findings. Prioritizing which to compile into wiki vs. leave as refactoring items required judgment calls.
- **Duplicate findings across audits** — The same issue (e.g., N+1 queries, missing Zod) appeared in multiple audits. Needed deduplication during wiki compilation.
- **Operational vs. architectural separation** — User requested wiki compilation of architectural patterns only, not operational/task-level information. Required filtering.
- **Wiki already had partial coverage** — Many findings were partially documented. Needed to identify the delta (what's new) rather than rewrite everything.

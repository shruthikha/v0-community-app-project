## What's New

Residents can now upload and share photos of their homes! Show off your garden, renovation progress, or favorite corner of your lot to the community.

## How It Works

1. Go to **Family Settings** in your dashboard
2. Scroll down to **Home Photos** section
3. Click to upload up to 10 photos of your home
4. Choose a hero photo to feature

No action needed for existing residents — the feature is now available in your settings.

## What Changed

- Added Home Photos section to Family Settings
- Lot details pages now display home photos
- Photos are stored securely in your personal lot folder

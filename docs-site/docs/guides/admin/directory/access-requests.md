# Handling Access Requests & New Members

This guide covers the workflow for bringing new residents into the community, either through public requests or manual invitations.

---

## Processing Access Requests

When potential residents request access via the community's landing page, they appear in the **Access Requests** tab.

![Resident Access Request Review](/screenshots/resident_access_request_admin_step_1.png)

### Review Workflow
1. **Verify**: Review the requester's legal name, email, and their intended **Lot assignment**.
2. **Approve**: Clicking "Approve" **does not** automatically create the account. Instead, it redirects you to the **Create Resident** form, pre-filled with the requester's information. This allows you to finalize their Lot and Family Unit mapping before the invitation is sent.
3. **Reject**: Declines the request. No record is created, and the requester is not automatically notified.

---

## Manually Adding Residents
Admins can also proactively add members to the directory.

1. Click **"Add Resident"** at the top of the directory table.
2. **Identity**: Provide names and email (leave blank for **Passive accounts**).
3. **Mapping**: Assign the resident to a specific **Lot** and **Family Unit**.

![Create Resident Step 1](/screenshots/resident_create_admin_step_1.png)

> [!TIP]
> Manual creation is ideal for children or household members who do not have their own email addresses (Passive Accounts).

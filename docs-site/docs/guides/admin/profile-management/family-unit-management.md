# Family Unit Management

While individual profile details are handled in the [Resident Profiles Guide](../directory/resident-profiles.md), Community Admins are responsible for managing the community's structural **Family Units**.

---

## 1. The Family Units Registry
Navigate to **Admin > Families** to view the structural list of all households. This view organizes individual residents and pets into their corresponding physical and logical groups.

![Family Units Registry](/screenshots/admin_families_list.png)

### Key Attributes
- **Family Name**: The logical identifier for the unit (e.g., "The Smith Family"). This name is visible to all household members.
- **Primary Contact**: Every unit must have one resident nominated as the main point of communication.
- **Unit Stats**: At-a-glance counts of total members and pets within the household.

---

## 2. Creating & Connecting Units
Admins define the household structure to ensure accurate community representation and emergency planning.

![Creating a Family Unit](/screenshots/admin_family_create.png)

### The Creation Workflow
1. **Name the Unit**: Provide a descriptive name for the household.
2. **Set Primary Contact**: Choose a resident from the directory to be the representative.
3. **Add Initial Members**: You can link residents to the unit during or after creation.

---

## 3. Structural Updates
Admins can adjust the composition and metadata of a Family Unit as the community evolves.

![Editing Family Connections](/screenshots/admin_family_edit.png)

- **Linking Residents**: To move a resident into or out of a Family Unit, use the **Edit Resident** form in the [Resident Profiles](../directory/resident-profiles.md) tab.
- **Primary Contact Updates**: Change the designated representative if the previous contact leaves or roles change.
- **Household Branding**: Update the Family Name or description as requested by the residents.

:::important
**Lot Assignment Note**: Physical **Lot assignments** are managed at the **Resident** level. If an entire household moves to a different physical residence, you must update the Lot field for each individual member in their respective profile edits.
:::

:::important
**Primary Contact Invariant**: A Family Unit cannot exist without a valid Primary Contact. Ensure a new representative is assigned before removing an existing one to prevent communication gaps.
:::

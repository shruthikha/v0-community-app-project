# Configuring Río AI: Persona and Tone

Administrators can tailor Río's "voice" to match the community's culture. These settings form **Tier 2** of the 3-Tier Prompt Model, overriding or supplementing Río's base instructions.

## Persona and Tone Settings

![Río Persona Config Part 1](/screenshots/rio_admin_agent_persona_step_1.png)
_Screenshot: Persona and Tone Settings_

### Configuration Fields:

- **Prompt Persona**: Define Río's specific character traits (e.g., "A formal concierge" vs. "A laid-back neighbor"). Keep this descriptive to help the AI understand its identity.
- **Tone**: Specify the desired communication style (e.g., "Professional yet warm," "Energetic," or "Concise").
- **Sign-off Message**: A mandatory phrase that Río will include at the end of its responses (e.g., "Have a wonderful day in the community!").

## Community Policies & Emergency Info

![Río Persona Config Part 2](/screenshots/rio_admin_agent_persona_step_2.png)
_Screenshot: Policies and Emergency Contacts_

- **Community Policies**: Highlight important local rules (e.g., "Quiet hours are 10 PM - 7 AM"). Río will prioritize these when answering community-related questions.
- **Emergency Contacts**: Provide local numbers (Security, Maintenance, Local Authorities) that Río should surface if it detects an urgent situation in the chat.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Persona changes are applied instantly to all new chat messages across the community.

# Creating a Request

To submit a new request, click the **+ New Request** button to open the submission wizard.

## Step-by-Step Wizard

### Step 1: Category
Choose the most relevant category for your issue.
*   **Maintenance**: Fixing or repair needed (e.g., broken street light).
*   **Safety**: Urgent safety concerns (e.g., wild animal sighting).
*   **Question**: General inquiries about community policies.
*   **Complaint**: Issues or concerns requiring mediation.
*   **Other**: Anything else not covered above.

![Category Selection](pathname:///screenshots/requests_resident_create_step_1.png)

### Step 2: Details
Provide a clear title and a detailed description.
Select a **Priority Level**:
*   **Normal**: Standard request, no immediate rush.
*   **Urgent**: Needs attention within a few days.
*   **Emergency**: Immediate danger or safety threat.

![Request Details](pathname:///screenshots/requests_resident_create_step_2.png)
![Request Details Continued](pathname:///screenshots/requests_resident_create_step_2(2).png)

### Step 3: Location & Photos
Adding context helps the administration resolve your issue faster.
*   **Location**: Pin the issue to a specific community location (like the Pool or Gym) or drop a custom pin on the map.
*   **Photos**: Upload up to 5 photos to visually document the issue.

![Context & Location](pathname:///screenshots/requests_resident_create_step_3.png)

### Step 4: Review & Privacy
Before submitting, review your details.
*   **Visibility**: Maintenance and Safety issues are visible to the entire community by default to ensure everyone stays informed. Complaints are always kept private between you and the administration.
*   **Anonymity**: For complaints, you can choose to submit anonymously.

![Final Review](pathname:///screenshots/requests_resident_create_step_4.png)

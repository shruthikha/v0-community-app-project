# Viewing Requests

The **Requests Overview** page provides a bird's-eye view of your own submissions and community-wide updates.

![Resident Overview](pathname:///screenshots/requests_resident_overview_1.png)

> [!TIP]
> For requests involving a specific place in the community, see the [Community Map guide](../map/viewer-basics.md).

## Filtering and Search

Use the filter cards at the top of the page to quickly find what you're looking for:
*   **Type**: Filter by Maintenance, Safety, Question, Complaint, etc.
*   **Status**: Focus on Pending, In Progress, or Resolved issues.
*   **Priority**: Highlight Emergency or Urgent matters.

## My Requests vs Community Requests

*   **My Requests**: Private tracking of your own issues.
*   **Community Requests**: Publicly visible maintenance or safety issues that affect everyone. You can join the conversation on these to provide additional context.

## Viewing Request Details

Clicking on any request card will open the **Request Details** view. This allows you to see the full description, history, and conversation thread.

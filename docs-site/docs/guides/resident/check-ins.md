# Check-ins

Check-ins are spontaneous "Neighbor Beacons" that let your community know where you are and what you're doing right now. They are ephemeral, meaning they only last as long as you're active.

---

## 📍 Create Check-in

To start a check-in, open the Create menu and select **Check-in**.

### The Creation Modal
The check-in form is a single-screen scrolling modal that allows you to quickly set your activity and visibility.

![Top of Modal](pathname:///screenshots/resident_checkin_modal_01(1).png)
![Middle of Modal](pathname:///screenshots/resident_checkin_modal_01(2).png)
![Bottom of Modal](pathname:///screenshots/resident_checkin_modal_01(3).png)

### Key Settings
- **Activity**: Choose an activity type (e.g., 'Coffee', 'Playing', 'Working').
- **Duration**: Set how long you plan to be active (from 30 minutes to 8 hours).
- **Location**: Use the map component to drop a marker or select a community location.
- **Interactions**: You can enable "Allow Responses" to let neighbors mark themselves as "Going" or "Maybe" to your check-in.
- **Visibility**: Like events, you can limit your beacon to your **Community**, specific **Neighborhoods**, or a Private list of **Residents, Families, or Neighbor Lists**.

---

## 🗺️ Respond to Check-in

### The Beacon Map
Active check-ins appear on the map as **pulsating markers** to indicate real-time presence. When a check-in has **less than 15 minutes** remaining, the time badge itself will start pulsating to signal that the activity is ending soon.

### Using the Side Panel
Click on any neighbor beacon to open the interaction side panel.

![Beacon Side Panel](pathname:///screenshots/resident_checkin_pulsating_beacon_03.png)

- **Status**: See what the neighbor is doing and how much time is left.
- **Respond**: Use the **Join** or **Maybe** buttons to signal your interest. 
    - **Join**: Tapping "Join" again will remove your RSVP (toggle-off).
    - Unlike scheduled events, there is no explicit "Not Going" button; you simply opt-out by not joining or removing your status.
- **Chat**: If enabled, you can quickly jump into a chat thread with the check-in creator.

> [!NOTE]
> Check-ins automatically expire when the duration ends. You can end your own check-in early from the side panel if your plans change.

# Managing Your Notifications

Stay up-to-date with community activities, exchange requests, and official announcements through your personalized notification center.

## Accessing Notifications

You can access your notifications by clicking the **Bell Icon** (🔔) in the top-right corner or the navigation sidebar. 
- A red dot on the bell indicates unread messages.
- For a full view, select **View All Notifications** from the dropdown or navigate to the Notifications page.

![Notification Bell](/screenshots/notifications_resident_bell_unread.png)

## What Triggers a Notification?

The system alerts you when something requires your attention or when a community event occurs:

- **Exchange Requests**: When someone requests to borrow your item, or when your request is confirmed/declined by a neighbor.
- **Community Requests**: Status updates on requests you have submitted (e.g., "In Progress" or "Resolved").
- **Announcements**: Official news published by community management.
- **Event Invites**: When you are invited to a community gathering or workshop.

## Managing Your Inbox

The Notifications page allows you to filter and act on your alerts:

### Tabs and Filtering
- **All**: Your complete history of notifications.
- **Exchange**: Alerts specifically related to the Community Exchange (borrows, returns, pickups).
- **Other**: System alerts and status updates.

![Notifications Sidebar Filters](/screenshots/notifications_page_overview.png)

### Reading and Archiving
- **Mark as Read**: Clicking a notification or selecting "Mark All as Read" will remove the unread indicator.
- **Archive**: To clear your view without deleting the history, hover over a notification and click the **Archive** button. This moves the alert to your archived tab.

![Archiving a Notification](/screenshots/notifications_archive_action.png)

## Action-Required Notifications

Some notifications are **interactive**. For example, in the Community Exchange:
- You may see **Confirm** or **Decline** buttons directly in the alert.
- These allow you to process requests without leaving the page.

![Exchange Actionable notification](/screenshots/notifications_exchange_actionable.png)

---

:::tip Pro-Tip
Use the **Mark All as Read** feature once a week to keep your dashboard clean and ensure you only see truly new alerts!
:::

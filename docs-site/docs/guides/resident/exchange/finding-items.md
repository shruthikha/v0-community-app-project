# Finding & Creating Items

The Exchange dashboard is your central hub for discovering what your neighbors are sharing and adding your own contributions to the community.

## 🔍 The Exchange Dashboard
When you land on the Exchange page, you are presented with a real-time feed of all active listings in your community.

![Exchange Landing Page](/screenshots/exchange_res_main_filters_1.png)
*The landing page features a powerful search bar and intuitive filter cards to help you find exactly what you need.*

### Search & Filtering
The Exchange includes a multi-layered filtering system to narrow down results:

- **Search**: Real-time keyword matching across titles and descriptions.
- **Categories**: Filter by "Household Items," "Food & Produce," or "Services & Skills."
- **Pricing Type**: Focus on "Free" items, "Fixed Price" listings, or "Pay What You Want" contributions.
- **Item Condition**: Sort by condition from "New" to "Used" or "Needs Maintenance."
- **Availability**: By default, only available items are shown. You can toggle "Include unavailable" to see past listings.
- **Neighborhood**: Filter listings to see what's specifically available in your block or area.

### Detailed View
Clicking any card opens the **Listing Modal**. As a browsing resident, this shows you the "Hero Photo," full description, the lender's bio, and a map of the pickup location.

![Listing Details Top](/screenshots/exchange_res_transaction_flow_1.png)
![Listing Details Bottom](/screenshots/exchange_res_listing_detail_creator_2.png)

## ➕ Creating a Listing
Ready to share something? Click the **"Share Something"** button to open the 5-step creation wizard.

### Step-by-Step Creation Wizard
1.  **Select Category**: Choose the exchange type (Item, Food, or Service).
2.  **Add Visuals & Info**: Upload a "Hero Photo" and write a clear description.
3.  **Pricing & Logistics**: Set your pricing model and the **Initial Quantity** available.
4.  **Visibility Scope**: Choose to share with your specific neighborhood or the entire community.
5.  **Final Review**: Review all details before publishing to the live dashboard.

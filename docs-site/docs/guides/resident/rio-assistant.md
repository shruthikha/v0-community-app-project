![Río Welcome Card](/screenshots/rio_welcome_card_step_1.png)
_Introducing Río: Your friendly neighborhood AI._

## 1. How to Chat with Río

You can start a conversation with Río anytime by clicking the **Río AI** button in your navigation or using the shortcut menu.

![Río Action Menu](/screenshots/rio_create_menu_step_1.png)
_Easy access via the create menu._

### Chat Features
- **Semantic Understanding**: Río remembers the context of your current conversation for coherent multi-turn chats.
- **Smart Formatting**: Río serves easy-to-read lists, bold text, and clickable links.

---

## 2. Using the Cheat Sheet

If you're not sure what to ask, Río provides a **Cheat Sheet** with common questions tailored to your community.

![Río Cheat Sheet](/screenshots/rio_cheat_sheet_step_1.png)

---

## 3. Knowledge & Citations

When Río finds information in community documents (like bylaws or facility guides), it provides **Inline Citations**. You can click these to see exactly which document the information came from.

![Río Citations UI](/screenshots/rio_citations_ui_step_1.png)
_Citations ensure transparency and trust._

---

## 4. Your Memory & Privacy

Río learns facts about your interests and preferences to provide personalized help. You have full control over what Río "remembers."

### Managing Your Fact Profile
You can view, edit, or delete any fact Río has learned about you in your [Privacy & Río Settings](./profile-management/privacy-rio-settings.md).

![Río Memory Settings](/screenshots/rio_memory_settings_step_1.png)

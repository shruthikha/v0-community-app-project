# Your Memory & Privacy

Río is designed to be helpful by learning your preferences and needs, but you are always in control of what it knows.

## How Río "Remembers"

Río learns helpful facts about you through your conversations, such as:
- Your interests or hobbies.
- Preferred languages.
- Community-related tasks you need help with.

## Managing Your Data

You have full sovereignty over your "Fact Profile." You can review and manage these memories in your **Privacy Settings**.

![Río Memory Settings](/screenshots/rio_memory_settings_step_1.png)

### What You Can Do:
1.  **Review**: See a list of everything Río currently "knows" about you.
2.  **Edit**: Correct any details that Río might have misunderstood.
3.  **Delete**: Remove specific facts or wipe your entire memory profile.

For a detailed walkthrough of how these settings interact with your overall profile visibility, see the [Privacy & Río Settings Guide](../profile-management/privacy-rio-settings.md).

> [!NOTE]
> **The Right to be Forgotten**: When you delete a memory, Río not only forgets it for future conversations but also redacts that information from your past chat history to ensure your privacy transition is complete.

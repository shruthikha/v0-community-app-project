# Using the Neighbor Directory

The Neighbor Directory is your portal to connecting with the people living around you.

![Neighbor Directory Dashboard](/screenshots/resident_directory_main_step_1.png)
*Figure 1: Main Neighbors Directory view.*

## Finding Neighbors

Navigate to **Neighbors** from your dashboard. The directory is organized into three primary views:

- **Residents**: An alphabetical list of individuals.
- **Households**: Neighbors grouped by Lot or Family Unit.
- **Search & Filters**: Use the global search bar for names/emails, or filter by Neighborhood and shared **Interests & Skills** (e.g., "Gardening" or "Tennis").

## resident Profiles

Clicking a neighbor's card opens their **Public Profile**.

![resident Profile Details](/screenshots/resident_profile_details_step_1.png)

### What You'll See:
- **Bio & Background**: "About" section, languages, and country of origin.
- **The Journey**: Move-in dates and construction status (e.g., Construction, Moved-in).
- **Interests**: Shared hobbies and expertise.

To manage what others see on your own profile, see the [Privacy & Río Settings Guide](../profile-management/privacy-rio-settings.md). To update your own details, visit the [Managing Your Profile Guide](../profile-management/managing-your-profile.md).

## Households & Pets

Nido shows the community as connected units:
- **Households**: View all family members linked to a Lot.
- **Pets**: See community animals, their profiles, and which household they belong to.

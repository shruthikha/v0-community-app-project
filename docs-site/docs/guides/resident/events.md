# Events

Stay connected with your community by discovering, attending, and creating events. 

---

## 🔍 Find Events

The Events dashboard provides a powerful set of tools to help you find activities that match your interests and schedule.

### View Options
- **List View**: A streamlined feed of upcoming events with dates and categories.
- **Calendar View**: A monthly grid ideal for viewing recurring series and planning long-term activities.

![Event List](pathname:///screenshots/resident_events_list.png)
![Calendar View](pathname:///screenshots/resident_events_calendar.png)

### Filter Options
Use the filter bar to narrow down the results across four key dimensions:
- **Category**: Filter by activity type (e.g., 'Social', 'Meeting', 'Fitness').
- **Location**: Show events at specific community spots or within your neighborhood.
- **Type**: Toggle between **Official Community Events** and **Resident Events**.
- **Time**: Filter by 'Today', 'This Week', or 'Upcoming' to see what's happening soon.

## ➕ Create Events

Sharing an event with your neighbors is a 4-step wizard process.

### Step 1: Basic Info
Provide a title, description, and upload a hero photo. Selecting a category here determines the icon that appears on the map and in the feed.

![Step 1: Basic Info](/screenshots/resident_events_create_info_02.png)

### Step 2: Timing & Series Logic
Set the start and end times for your event. 

- **Event Series**: Check "This is a recurring event" to open advanced scheduling. You can set events to repeat Daily, Weekly, or Monthly. 
    - *Note*: Editing a single occurrence of a series later will "detach" it from the master schedule.
- **RSVPs**: Enable RSVPs to track attendance. You can set a **Max Capacity** and an **RSVP Deadline** (e.g., 24 hours before the event starts).

![Step 2: Timing](/screenshots/resident_events_create_timing_03(2).png)

### Step 3: Location Tagging
Select a predefined location or drop a custom marker. 
- **Map Preview**: As you select or draw a location (Point or Polygon), the map preview updates in real-time to show exactly where your neighbors should go.

![Step 3: Location](/screenshots/resident_events_create_location_04.png)

### Step 4: Visibility & Invites
Choose who can see and join your event:
- **Community Wide**: Visible to all residents in your community.
- **Neighborhood**: Restricted to residents of selected neighborhoods.
- **Private**: This mode restricts visibility to specific invitees. You can invite:
    - **Residents**: Individual neighbors.
    - **Families**: Entire family units.
    - **Lists**: Custom groups you've created in your Neighbor Directory.

![Step 4: Visibility](/screenshots/resident_events_create_invites_05(3).png)

## 📄 Event Details Page

The Details Page is the central hub for any event. 

![Event Details](pathname:///screenshots/resident_events_details_page_06(1).png)

### Interaction Options
- **RSVP**: Respond with **Attending**, **Maybe**, or **Not Attending**. Your response is shared with the organizer and other residents (unless marked private).
- **Save**: Click the Heart icon to add the event to your personal shortlist.
- **Share**: Use the share button to copy a link (Mobile/Web) to the event.
- **Management**: If you are the creator, you can **Edit** or **Cancel** the event directly from this page.

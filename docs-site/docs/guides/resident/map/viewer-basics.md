# Map Viewer Basics

The Community Map provides an interactive 3D view of your community's facilities, lots, and neighborhoods.

![Community Map Overview](/screenshots/map_resident_overview_01.png)

## Accessing the Map
Find the **Community Map** link in your main navigation or visit `/dashboard/community-map`. 

## Controls & Navigation
- **Zoom**: Use your mouse wheel or pinch-to-zoom.
- **Tilt/Rotate**: Right-click and drag (desktop) or two-finger drag (mobile).
- **Geolocate 🎯**: Centers the map on your current physical location.
- **Fullscreen ⛶**: Expands the map to fill your entire screen.

## Map Layers & Filters
Use the sidebar to toggle visibility for different types of locations:

![Map Categories](/screenshots/map_filters_sidebar_02.png)

- **🗺️ Boundary**: The community's perimeter.
- **🏡 Lots**: Individual property parcels.
- **🏛️ Facilities**: Common areas, parks, and clubhouses.
- **🛣️ Streets**: Internal road networks.
- **🚶 Paths**: Walking and hiking trails.
- **📍 Check-ins**: Real-time location of active neighbors.

# Location Details & Integrated Maps

The map is deeply integrated with the rest of the community platform, serving as the central hub for spatial information.

## Location Detail Cards
Clicking any location on the map opens a detailed side panel with context-specific information.

### Facilities & Amenities
Shows the current status, capacity, and a checklist of available amenities (e.g., WiFi, Restrooms).
![Facility Details](/screenshots/map_location_card_facility_03.png)

### Resident Lots
Displays the household name, registered residents, and links to their public profiles.
![Lot Details](/screenshots/map_location_card_lot_04.png)

### Walking Paths
Shows the total distance, difficulty rating, and surface type calculation.
![Path Details](/screenshots/map_location_card_path_05.png)

## Integrated Mini-Maps
You don't always have to be on the main map page to see spatial data. Small map snippets are embedded in:
- **Resident Profiles**: Shows where a neighbor lives.
- **Events**: Shows the exact meeting spot or venue.
- **The Exchange**: Shows where items are located for pickup.

![Profile Map Embed](/screenshots/map_resident_profile_embed_01.png)
*Map snippet on a resident's profile.*

![Event Map Embed](/screenshots/map_event_location_embed_02.png)
*Location context for a community event.*

**Pro Tip**: Clicking any mini-map will take you to the main Community Map with that location automatically selected and the detail card open.

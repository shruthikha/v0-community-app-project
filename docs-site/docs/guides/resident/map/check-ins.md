# Real-time Check-ins

See where your community is active in real-time through the map-based check-in system.

## Neighbor Beacons
When you or your neighbors check into a facility or path, a "Beacon" containing your profile picture appears at that location on the map.

> [!NOTE]
> Check-in visibility depends entirely on individual privacy settings. If a neighbor has their profile set to "Private", their beacon will not appear.

## Dynamic Grouping
If multiple residents are checked into the same location (like the clubhouse), the map intelligently spreads the beacons out in a "spider" pattern so every individual is visible without overlapping.

## How to Check In
While the map displays current check-ins, you can perform your own check-ins via:
1. The **Check-in** button on the main dashboard.
2. The **Scan QR** feature at physical locations.
3. Clicking a location on the map and selecting **Check In Here**.

# 3D High-Fidelity Mode

Experience your community in a stunning, immersive 3D environment that highlights architecture, terrain, and landmarks.

![3D Map Mode](/screenshots/map_3D.png)

## Immersive Visualization
The Community Map uses height data and building footprints to render the landscape in three dimensions. This mode is particularly useful for:
- Understanding the slope and elevation of **Walking Paths**.
- Visualizing property boundaries in relation to natural landmarks.
- Getting a realistic sense of facility layouts.

## Controls for 3D
To get the most out of 3D mode:
1. **Tilt the camera**: Hold the right mouse button and drag up/down.
2. **Rotate**: Drag left/right while holding the right mouse button.
3. **Dynamic Shadows**: The map simulates realistic lighting based on the time of day, making 3D features stand out clearly with depth and shadows.

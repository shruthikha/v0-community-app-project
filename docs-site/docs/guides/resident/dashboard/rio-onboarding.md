# Resident Guide: Onboarding with Río

Río is your community guide. When you first join, Río helps you set up your profile and understand how everything works.

![Rio Welcome Card](/screenshots/rio_welcome_card_step_1.png)

## The Onboarding Tour
The tour is a 10-step interactive walkthrough of the app's features.
- **How to Start**: Click "Start Tour" in the Río widget on your dashboard.
- **What You'll Learn**:
  - Setting your theme (light/dark).
  - How the community map works.
  - Participating in events and check-ins.
  - Using the community exchange.
  - Submitting service requests.

## Completing Your Profile
A complete profile helps neighbors get to know you better and allows Río to provide more personalized assistance.

### Profile Steps
1. **Personal Info**: Share your name, birthday, and origin.
2. **Family & Pets**: Introduce your family members and furry friends.
3. **Interests**: Select topics you're interested in to find like-minded neighbors.
4. **Skills**: Share what you're good at and decide if you're open to neighbors requesting your help.

### Why Complete Your Profile?
- **Better Suggestions**: Río can suggest events and groups that match your interests.
- **Community Connection**: Neighbors can find you if you share a skill they need help with.

## Asking Río Anything

If Río AI is enabled, you can type questions directly into the search bar on the welcome card.
- **Example Questions**:
  - "When is the next community dinner?"
  - "How do I book the woodshop?"
  - "Who has a drill I can borrow?"

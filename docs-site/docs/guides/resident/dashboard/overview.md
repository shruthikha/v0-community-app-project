# Resident Guide: Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard is your community's central hub. From here, you can see what's happening, track your tasks, and interact with Río, your community guide.

## Dashboard Layout

![Dashboard Overview](/screenshots/dashboard_full_overview.png)
*Your community hub at a glance.*

### 1. Welcome Section (Top Left)
- **Greeting**: Personal welcome message.
- **Río Widget**: Start your community tour, complete your profile, or ask Río anything.
- **Actions**: Quick buttons for onboarding and profile management.

### 2. What's Next (Top Right)
- **Priority Feed**: A score-based list of the most important things for you to know right now.
- **Actionable Items**: Upcoming event check-ins, exchange transaction updates, and community announcements.

### 3. Quick Stats (Center)
- **Key Metrics**: A grid of 4 customizable stats (e.g., Upcoming Events, Active Listings).
- **Personalized**: Highlight the numbers that matter most to you.

### 4. Explore & Catch-up (Bottom)
- **Announcements**: Browse latest community updates.
- **Events**: See what's coming up and RSVP.
- **Live Check-ins**: See who is currently active in the community.
- **My Reservations**: Manage your facility bookings.
- **My Listings & Transactions**: Track your exchange items.
- **My Active Requests**: Monitor your service and maintenance tickets.
- **Our Neighborhood**: Explore the interactive community map.

## Getting Around
- Use the **"View All"** links to dive deeper into any section.
- Most items can be interacted with directly from the dashboard for quick actions (like RSVPing or confirming a pickup).

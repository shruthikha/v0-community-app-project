# Resident Guide: Explore & Catch-up Widgets

The Explore section is where you can dive into specific community features through dedicated widgets.

## The Widgets

![Explore & Catch-up Widgets](/screenshots/dashboard_explore_widgets.png)
*A unified view of everything happening in your community.*

### 1. Community Announcements
Shows recent updates from community management. Unread items are highlighted with a priority badge.

### 2. My Upcoming Events
Lists the next few events. You can RSVP "Yes" or "No", or "Save" an event for later directly from the widget.

### 3. Live Check-ins
A real-time view of who is currently active. You can see where they are and click "View" to see more details or join them.

### 4. My Reservations
Manage your bookings for community facilities (like the woodshop or guest house). You can cancel upcoming reservations here.

### 5. My Listings & Transactions
Track your activity in the community exchange.
- **Listings**: Manage your shared items (Pause, Publish, Delete).
- **Transactions**: See the status of items you are borrowing or lending.
- **Archive**: View your history of completed transactions.

### 6. My Active Requests
Track your maintenance or service tickets. See status updates (Pending, In-progress) and click to view full details or comments.

### 7. Our Neighborhood (Map)
An interactive map showing your lot, your neighbors' lots, and where active check-ins are occurring. Click "View All" for the full-screen map experience.

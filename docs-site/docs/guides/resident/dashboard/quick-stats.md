# Resident Guide: Quick Stats

Quick Stats give you a pulse on what's happening in your community and your personal engagement.

## Customizing Your Stats
![Quick Stats Grid](/screenshots/quick_stats_grid.png)

You can choose which 4 stats to display on your dashboard. 

### How to Edit
![Edit Stats Modal](/screenshots/quick_stats_edit_modal.png)

1. Locate the **Quick Stats** section on your dashboard.
2. Click the **"Edit Stats"** button (next to the section title).
3. Select up to 4 stats from the library.
4. Click **"Save Changes"**.

## Available Stats Library

### Community Stats
- **# Upcoming Events**: Events happening in the next 7 days.
- **# Community Announcements**: Active community updates.
- **# Available Listings**: Total items available in the community exchange.
- **# Neighbors**: Total active community members.
- **# Currently At**: Neighbors currently checked in at specific locations.

### My Stats
- **# My Reservations**: Your upcoming facility bookings.
- **# My Active Listings**: Items you currently have listed for share.
- **# My Active Requests**: Your pending service or maintenance tickets.
- **# My Available Balance**: Your current community credit (if enabled).
- **# My Upcoming RSVPs**: Total events you are attending.

## Why Stats Matter
Stats allow you to monitor community growth and your own activity at a glance. For example, seeing "0 Available Listings" might prompt you to share something new!

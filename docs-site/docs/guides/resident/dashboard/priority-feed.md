# Resident Guide: The Priority Feed ("What's Next")

The Priority Feed is a smart list that helps you stay on top of the most important community activities.

## How it Works
![What's Next Feed](/screenshots/priority_feed_items.png)
*Stay updated with high-priority community activities.*

Río automatically scores community updates based on their importance to you. The high-scoring items appear at the top of your feed.

### Common Feed Items
- **Exchange Requests**: When someone wants to borrow an item you listed.
- **Return Reminders**: When an item you borrowed is due today.
- **Unread Announcements**: Important updates from the community manager.
- **Check-ins**: When a neighbor is active and has shared their location.
- **Upcoming Events**: Reminders for events you have RSVP'd to.

## Interacting with the Feed
- **Quick Actions**: Many items have buttons (like "Confirm Pickup" or "RSVP") that you can click without leaving the dashboard.
- **Dismissing Items**: Click the **"X"** icon in the top right of an item to hide it from your feed until you return to the dashboard.
- **View All**: Click "View All" in any feed section to see a full history or more items.

## Customization
The priority feed dynamically adjusts based on your activity. The more you interact with the community, the smarter Río gets at showing you exactly what you need.

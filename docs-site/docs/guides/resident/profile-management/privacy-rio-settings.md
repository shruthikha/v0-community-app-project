# Privacy & Río Settings

Maintaining control over your personal data is a core pillar of our community. This guide explains how to manage your directory visibility and your data sovereignty with the Río AI assistant.

---

## Privacy Toggles
Navigate to **Settings > Privacy** to define exactly what information is shared with your neighbors.

![Contact & Personal Settings](/screenshots/resident_privacy_settings_1.png)

![Journey & Community Settings](/screenshots/resident_privacy_settings_2.png)

### Visibility Groups
Information is categorized into logical groups. You can toggle the visibility of individual fields within each group:

- **Contact Information**: Toggle Email Address and Phone Number.
- **Personal Details**: Manage visibility for Birthday, Country of Origin, Current Country, and Languages.
- **Journey Information**: Share or hide your Journey Stage, Move-in Date, and Construction Dates.
- **Community & Family**: Control visibility for Family Members, Interests, and Skills.

:::important
**Public Safety Default**: Your name, profile picture, lot number, and neighborhood are always visible to other residents to ensure community safety and identification.
:::

---

## Río AI Memory Management
Río, your AI assistant, remembers important details from your profile and chats to provide a personalized experience. You have absolute control over this memory.

![Río Memory Settings](/screenshots/resident_privacy_settings_3.png)

### Managing Learned Facts
Within the **Río AI Memory** section, you can see a live gallery of "Facts" Río has learned about you. These are categorized into:
- **Identity & Context**: Location, Occupation (Skills), Projects.
- **Personal Preferences**: Interests, Events, Facts.

### Memory Sovereignty
- **Edit/Delete Individual Facts**: Use the edit (pencil) or delete (trash) icons on any memory card to adjust or remove specific information.
- **Clear All Memories**: Use the **Wipe Memory** function to permanently delete everything Río has learned about you. This requires typing "DELETE" to confirm.

:::note
**Security & Isolation**: Río's memories are encrypted and isolated to your specific resident profile. No other resident or admin can access your personal AI memory.
:::

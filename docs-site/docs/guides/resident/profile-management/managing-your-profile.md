# Managing Your Profile

Your resident profile is your calling card in the community. It allows you to share your interests, languages, skills, and journey stage while maintaining control over how your information is saved.

---

## Accessing the Profile Editor

Navigate to **Settings > Profile** from your resident dashboard.

![Profile Identity & Journey](/screenshots/resident_profile_edit_1.png)

![Personal Details & Skills](/screenshots/resident_profile_edit_2.png)

### 1. Identity & Contact
- **First & Last Name**: Your primary identification in the directory.
- **Email**: Managed by your Community Admin. To change this, please contact your neighborhood representative.
- **Phone Number**: Optional. If provided, it can be shared with neighbors according to your [Privacy Settings](./privacy-rio-settings.md).

### 2. My Journey
Track your progress in the community by selecting your current **Journey Stage**:
- **Planning**: Design and preparation phase.
- **Building**: Construction is underway.
- **Arriving**: Preparing for the move.
- **Integrating**: Living in the community and getting settled.

You can also specify your **Estimated Move-in Date** and the span of your **Construction Schedule**.

### 3. Personal Details & Languages
- **About**: A text area to tell neighbors about yourself.
- **Birthday & Origin**: Share your birthday (visibility can be restricted) and where you are from.
- **Languages**: List all languages you speak and select your **Preferred Language** for community interactions.

### 4. Interests & Skills
- **Interests**: Search for existing interests or create a new one to find like-minded neighbors.
- **Skills**: Select from the community skill bank or add custom skills. 
- **Open to Help**: Use the toggle beside each skill to signal if you're available to assist neighbors (e.g., "Open to Help" with Gardening).

---

## Photo Gallery
Share photos of yourself, your life, and your journey.
- **Hero Photo**: Select one image from your gallery to be your primary profile picture (starred).
- **Gallery**: Upload up to 10 photos to show more of your resident story.

---

## Silent Auto-save

The profile editor uses a **Silent Auto-save** mechanism. There is no manual "Save" button for the majority of fields.

- **How it works**: Every change you make is saved automatically as soon as you move focus away from a field (`onBlur`) or select a new value from a dropdown.
- **Visual Confirmation**: Watch for the **Save Status** indicator (e.g., "Saving..." then "Saved") to confirm your change has been persisted.

:::tip
Since saves are automatic, you can leave the profile editor at any time without fear of losing your progress.
:::

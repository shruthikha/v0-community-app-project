# Authentication & Access

This guide covers how residents gain and maintain access to the community application, from the initial invitation to secure login and password recovery.

---

## Gaining Access

There are two primary ways to join the community:

### 1. Invitation (Admin-Led)
If a Community Admin adds you to the directory, you will receive an invitation email containing a secure link.
- **The Link**: Click the "Set Up Account" link to verify your identity.
- **Set Password**: You will be directed to a secure page to establish your personal password. 
- **Activation**: Once your password is saved, your account transitions from `Invited` to `Active`, and you can proceed to log in.

### 2. Access Request (Resident-Led)
If you are a new resident not yet in the system, you can request access directly from the login page.

![Access Request Form](/screenshots/auth_request_access.png)

- **Entry**: Provide your legal name, email address, and assigned lot number.
- **Vetting**: Your request will be reviewed by the Community Admin.
- **Onboarding**: Once approved, you will receive the standard invitation email to set your password and complete your profile.

---

## Secure Login

Access the community at any time via the main login portal. Ensure you are logging into your specific neighborhood's URL to maintain tenant context.

![Login Page](/screenshots/auth_login_main.png)

- **Credentials**: Use your registered email address and the password you established during onboarding.
- **Persistence**: You can choose to stay logged in on trusted personal devices.

---

## Password Management

### Forgotten Passwords
If you cannot sign in, use the **Forgot Password?** link on the login page to trigger a recovery email.

![Password Reset](/screenshots/auth_password_reset.png)

1. Enter your registered email address.
2. Click the secure link in the recovery email.
3. Establish a new password and log in immediately.

### Security Best Practices
- **Unique Passwords**: Avoid reusing passwords from other sensitive accounts.
- **Session Security**: Always log out when using shared or public devices.

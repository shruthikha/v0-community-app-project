# Household Management

The **Household** settings allow you to manage the private group of people and pets living with you, as well as the shared identity of your living unit.

---

## Shared Household Profile
Unlike your personal profile, the **Household Profile** represents your entire unit in the community directory.

![Household Manager](/screenshots/resident_household_manage.png)

- **Household Profile**: Update your **Family Name** (contact admin to change) and provide a **Family Description** (e.g., "The local hiking enthusiasts").
- **Household Photos**: Upload a **Banner Image** for your household and manage a gallery of shared photos that showcase your unit's story.

---

## Managing Members
Navigate to the **Family Members** section within Household settings to view and edit the people in your unit.

### Adding & Inviting Members
Residents can add new members (e.g., family or co-habitants) to their household registry.
- **Active Members**: Provide an email address to send an **Invitation Link**. Once the person accepts, they will have their own app access and personal profile.
- **Passive Members**: Leave the email address blank to create a **Passive Account**. This is ideal for children or others who do not need independent app access but should still be represented in the community directory and emergency records.

### Requesting Access
If a member is currently a passive account, you can convert them to an active member by editing their entry and providing a valid email address. This will trigger the standard invitation workflow.

---

## Managing Pets
The **Household Pets** registry serves as the primary record for community animals. Ensure your pet's name, species, breed, and photo are current to assist neighbors and security in identification.
